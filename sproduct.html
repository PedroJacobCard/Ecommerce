Product Details

When baked, croissants have a crispy exterior and a soft, flaky interior. They are often enjoyed for breakfast or as a snack, and can be served plain or filled with chocolate, almond paste, or other sweet or savory fillings. Croissants have become a beloved pastry all over the world, but they remain a symbol of French cuisine and culture. They are a testament to the French tradition of taking simple ingredients and transforming them into something truly extraordinary.
